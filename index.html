About Our Server

We’re a Discord community devoted to Gokurakugai, the action-supernatural manga written and illustrated by Yuto Sano. Originally introduced as a one-shot titled Gokurakugai Sanbandori No Ken in Jump SQ.RISE Winter 2020, the series officially began serialization on July 4, 2022.

By joining our Discord server, you agree to our server rules and Discord’s Terms of Service.
